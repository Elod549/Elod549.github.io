Our Story

It all started in February 2023 in an old disused cable factory in Delft in the Netherlands when a group of musicians with a passion for the blues got together to form a band.
Their mission? To keep the blues alive.

The following months are spent practising hard, doing a few try-out gigs and searching for the right vibe, the right sound. At the end of the summer it finally all comes together, the CableHouse blues band is ready to roll.
In October a gig in Germany is organised to put it all to the test. It works! Big smiles all round. It feels good. The band rocks!

2024
Time to hit the road, bringing the show to pubs, clubs, festivals and radio. Electric blues played from the heart. Chicago grooves, Texas shuffles, London beats, played with spirit, with passion, keeping it real. Keeping the blues alive.

2025
The band goes from strength to strength, gelling, becoming a well oiled blues machine. More gigs are played and a new rehearsal space is furbished in the cable factory. The band works on new material.

2026
The story continues.

CableHouse is:

  • Jos Waal - Drums
  • Manolo Chincola Sanchez - Bass
  • Kees van Steenbruggen - Keyboards
  • Freek Carels - Guitar
  • Ian Dole - Vocals & Harmonica

Damn right we've got the blues!
